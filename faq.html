FAQ

Common Questions Before Starting

Simple answers about paid ads, funnels, budgets, landing pages, and WhatsApp follow-up.

Do you only run Meta ads?

Meta Ads is a core service, but the work also covers funnels, landing pages, WhatsApp follow-up, tracking, and sales handover.

Do you work with small businesses?

Yes. The audit and setup packages are designed for small teams that need clarity before scaling spend.

Do you work in both Abu Dhabi and Dubai?

Yes. The positioning covers Abu Dhabi, Dubai, and UAE-based service-area businesses.

What budget do I need for ads?

A useful test budget depends on your industry, offer, and sales value. For many local services, start with enough to test several creatives and audiences.

Can you guarantee results?

No ethical marketer can guarantee exact results. I focus on building a clear campaign structure, testing creatives, improving lead quality, tracking performance, and making data-based decisions.

Do you create ad creatives?

I can plan creative angles, hooks, copy, and testing direction. Final production can be handled with your designer or a production partner.

Do you build landing pages?

The MVP offer covers landing page strategy and structure. Full build support can be added depending on platform and scope.

Do you help with WhatsApp follow-up?

Yes. This includes auto-replies, follow-up messages, qualification flow, objections, booking scripts, and team templates.

How long does it take to see results?

Most campaigns need a testing period. Early signals can appear quickly, but stronger decisions come after enough traffic, leads, and follow-up data.

What do you need before starting?

Access to current ads, landing pages, tracking, lead flow, offer details, and your sales process helps make the audit useful.
